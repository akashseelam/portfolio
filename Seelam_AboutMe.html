Akash Seelam

My name is Akash Seelam and I am a freshman at Dublin High School. I am aspiring, as my ultimate goal, to be a marine biologist. To reach this goal, I would like to attend the University of Marine Biology in Hawaii. In this letter, I would like to tell you a little bit about myself. I am a hard worker, can be a group leader or work alone, and will always get an amazing project in before the deadline. I am a multipotentialite, and have experience in a variety of fields. A few things about me: I play sports, I like to hike, swim, and study.I am a perfectionist, as one time in 8th grade, the most important project of the year, which took up the majority of our second semester, was looming. Our group was mediocre, even though we were all friends.Our group squabbled, argued, and fought over the smallest things, as who gets to write their name first on the board. The board was a disaster. I tried reasoning with my teammates, but they insisted they will get it done. In the last week, I was finished with the bickering. I went up to ask my teacher to switch groups, and I was unfortunate not to have done it sooner. In the last three days, I finished the project (Starting from scratch) that was supposed to be done by six people over the course of three months. Later, I got my grade back from my teacher. It was an A. My former group got a C-. This shows that while I can work hard, can get good work done in a short amount of time, and I can make good choices for my education, as I left my friends for a better grade. However, I can work in a group very well too. In 7th grade, we had a similar project. However, I was put in a team with responsible individuals, and we worked day and night. We quite literally outshone all the competition. We presented, and our teacher was amazed that 5 seventh graders came up with a product so organized and well-presented. Our project was so good that she insisted she keep it as an example of a perfect project. On that project, we got a 110 percent. We got extra credit for how good the project was, and we were in the spotlight. I love to work on something that I like to do. I am responsible, hardworking, and also a team player, things that are important in the world we have today. I always pursue what I love to the fullest extent, as I am curious and what to learn more about the world I live in. I can make smart choices for my future even when I am potentially risking a friendship.Thank you for your time.
