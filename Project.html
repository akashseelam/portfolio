Saturday Night Drive

DHS Freshman Year

This is a straightforward game created by my partner Hagan and I during my freshman year at Dublin High School. In this game, you operate as a car trying to get valuable gems as they keep teleporting over a set amount of time. Also in this game, you must not hit the people, as each will make you lose points. In this project, I worked on the code while Hagan helped, and we both contributed to the documentation.
